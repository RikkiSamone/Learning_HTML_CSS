Naya Rivera

1987 - 2020

Naya Rivera was a beloved actress, singer, and advocate whose talent and radiant personality touched countless lives. Best known for her portrayal of Santana Lopez on Glee, Naya broke boundaries with her unapologetically bold and heartfelt performance as a queer Latina, inspiring many to embrace their true selves.

Beyond her artistry, Naya was a devoted mother whose love for her son, Josey, was evident in every aspect of her life. Her compassion extended beyond her personal circle—she used her platform to support causes she believed in, including LGBTQ+ rights and mental health awareness.

Her sudden passing in 2020 was a profound loss, yet her legacy continues to resonate. Naya’s courage, talent, and humanity left an indelible mark on her fans, peers, and the entertainment world.

Her voice—both figuratively and literally—was a beacon of authenticity and power. Whether belting out a show-stopping number on Glee or sharing candid moments with her fans, Naya reminded us of the importance of love, resilience, and self-expression.

We remember her for her artistry, strength, and the joy she brought to the world. Rest in peace, Naya Rivera—you are deeply missed and forever cherished.
