Menu

  • Bulgolgi BBQ

    Thinly sliced beef marinated in a blend of soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and a touch of sweetness, grilled to perfection. Served with steamed rice and a side of fresh vegetables.

  • Kimchee Jigae

    A hearty and spicy stew made with aged kimchi, tofu, pork, and green onions simmered in a rich broth. Served bubbling hot with a side of rice.

  • Bibimbap

    A vibrant bowl of warm rice topped with seasoned vegetables, a fried egg, and your choice of beef, chicken, or tofu. Served with gochujang (spicy red chili paste) for mixing.

  • Tteokbokki

    Soft and chewy rice cakes stir-fried in a bold and spicy gochujang-based sauce, paired with fish cakes and boiled eggs for a comforting street-food favorite.

  • Haemul Pajeon

    A savory Korean pancake packed with green onions, shrimp, squid, and mussels, pan-fried until golden and crispy. Served with a tangy soy dipping sauce.
